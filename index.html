about

Ryan Mugan is a graduate of the University of Wisconsin – Eau Claire where he completed a large array of technical, business, and design oriented courses. In addition to Ryan's time in the classroom, he explored a number of web-based technologies such as responsive frameworks, MVC design patterns, content management systems, and various JavaScript libraries.

Ryan's professional work experience includes an assortment of technical positions at JAMF Software, 3M, UW - Eau Claire, and currently Liberty Mutual Insurance. Ryan is working within Liberty’s Technical Development Program with the goal of building business, technical, project management and leadership skills.
